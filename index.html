Weill Cornell Data Science Club

About Us

The Weill Cornell Data Science Club is a student organization focused on encouraging the exploration the application of data science both inside and outside the medical realm. The goals for this club also include fostering cross-institutional interaction and helping members grow personally and professionally through cultivating a skillset in data science. We encourage students of all backgrounds and experience levels to attend the events.

Club activities include regular meetings where students share and guest speaker events where professors and alumni from Weill Cornell Medical campus as well as the Ithaca Campus will present on a variety of topics.
