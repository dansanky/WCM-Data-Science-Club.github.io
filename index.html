WCM Data Science Club

About Us

The Weill Cornell Data Science Club is a student organization focused on encouraging the exploration the application of data science both inside and outside the medical realm. The goals for this club also include fostering cross-institutional interaction and helping members grow personally and professionally with the computational skills they wish to master.
